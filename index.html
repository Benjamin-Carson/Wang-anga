About Our School

Building Tomorrow's Leaders Today

Our Story

Wang'anga Senior School is a leading institution dedicated to providing quality education to senior secondary school students in Kenya. We believe in nurturing well-rounded individuals who are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and values to succeed in life.

Our Mission

To provide holistic education that develops intellectually competent, morally upright, and socially responsible citizens who contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To be a center of academic excellence producing globally competitive graduates.
